# Clipus SaaS Copy

Write conversion-focused B2B SaaS copy grounded in the real product. English only (US market).

## Principles
- **Real:** every claim maps to an actual capability or proof point. No invented metrics.
- **Specific over clever:** concrete outcomes beat wordplay.
- **One job per section:** hero states the core value; each feature block earns its place.

## Output
- 3 headline options (specific, benefit-led, ≤ 10 words each).
- 1 subhead expanding the chosen headline (≤ 20 words).
- Up to 3 feature blurbs (name + one-line concrete benefit).
- 1 primary CTA + 1 secondary CTA.

## Self-check
- Could a competitor copy-paste this and have it be equally true? If yes, it's too generic — sharpen it.
- Is every number/claim sourced from the real product?
